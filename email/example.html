Making Amazon packaging more sustainable
 
Across the globe, we continue to find ways to reduce our packaging, while making the materials we use easier to recycle. For example, in 2023, we shipped 12% of our orders globally without any additional Amazon packaging—just a shipping label. We’ve also removed all plastic air pillows from packaging used at our global fulfillment centers.
