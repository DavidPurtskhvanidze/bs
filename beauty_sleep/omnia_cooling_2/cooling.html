how is the sleep&glow different from other pillows?

Regular
pillow
Orthopedic
pillow

When you sleep on a regular pillow (no matter the pillowcase material), your skin may become stretched and compressed, creating creases and morning puffiness. Over time, they turn into permanent sleep wrinkles.

Thanks to its special design, the Sleep&Glow provides comfort while reducing contact between the face and pillow, preventing and fighting sleep wrinkles and morning puffiness.

The technology

Ventilation perforating
technology

The pillow's ventilation zones prevent heat buildup. The location of the holes is concentrated around the cheeks and eyes — the areas most susceptible to swelling

Innovative Thermoregulating
pillowcase

Our unique material, with paraffin microencapsulated into the fabric, has special thermoregulating properties that ensure an ideal microclimate.

BENEFITS OF COOLING

deep beauty sleep

Overheating is 1 of the top 3 contributing factors in reduced sleep quality. Our pillow creates the perfect microclimate for sleep and supports optimal body temperature all night long.

SOOTHES SKIN

Nighttime heat causes the skin to become over- moisturized, leading to irritation and imperfections. The innovative Omnia Cooling absorbs excess sweat, preventing overheating.

REDUCED PUFFINESS

Poor sleep, overheating and bad pillow posture are all sources of morning puffiness. Avoid them all with our new product, the Omnia Cooling, with a special sloped design and thermoregulating technology so you wake up glowing.​
